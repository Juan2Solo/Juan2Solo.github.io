/Basque

The Basque people are indigenous to parts of Spain and France. In fact, the Basque language is unrelated to any other known language, which has led some to believe that the Basque people are the last surviving remnants of the Stone Age. The Basque people have a long history of independence, and even today there are separatist movements in both Spain and France.
